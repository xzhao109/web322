Introduction

“Real-world” experience is a great way to reinforce classroom material. It brings to life the things you learn and gives them a chance to breathe with real-world applications. It also gives you a chance to appreciate both the importance and the limitation of theory ... not everything works out as neatly as it says in the book! But with a solid scientific foundation, you are better able to evaluate and adapt, gaining greater insight into the process of software.
